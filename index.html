Joyas Baron

Type

Ecommerce

Date

30.04.2024

Role

UI/UX designer
Web Designer
SEO Specialist
Marketing Coordinator

Client

Joyas Baron

As the UX/UI designer, I led the redesign of the e-commerce platform to enhance user experience and visual appeal. The project involved optimizing product listings, creating sales campaigns, and collaborating with internal teams to align design with business objectives. Intensive work was done on SEO: creating blogs, meta tags, meta titles, meta descriptions, and configuring Google Search Console to position relevant keywords and improve search engine rankings. Continuous support was also provided for maintenance and design updates.

The challenge

When I joined the project, Joyas Barón faced significant challenges in user engagement and visibility. The website had a high bounce rate of approximately 50%, with users leaving the site quickly due to poor user experience and unoptimized product pages. Additionally, the site’s visibility in search engine rankings was low, frequently appearing in 6th or 7th place, limiting organic traffic and overall reach. The primary goals were to reduce the bounce rate, increase the time users spent on the site, and improve the website’s ranking to boost visibility and drive more conversions.

My solution

To tackle the high bounce rate and improve user experience, the website underwent a comprehensive redesign focused on creating a more intuitive and streamlined interface. The checkout process, which previously involved too many steps, was simplified to minimize user frustration and reduce abandonment. Product descriptions, photos, and titles were optimized to enhance clarity and appeal, while the navigation menus were restructured for easier browsing. In addition to improving the overall design, SEO strategies were implemented to boost the site’s visibility in search engine rankings. These changes not only improved usability but also played a key role in elevating Joyas Barón from lower-ranked positions (6th or 7th) to consistently appearing at the top of search results.
